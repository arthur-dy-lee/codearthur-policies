Frequently Asked Questions

How does silence detection work?

SilenceCut analyzes your audio track to find segments below a volume threshold. You can adjust the sensitivity using the volume threshold, minimum silence duration, and padding controls.

Is my data uploaded to any server?

No. All audio and video processing happens entirely on your device. Your files never leave your iPhone or iPad.

What is SilenceCut Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited projects, watermark-free exports, and an ad-free experience. You can subscribe monthly or yearly from the app.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open SilenceCut, go to the subscription page, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

What export formats are supported?

Video: MP4, MOV (H.264). Audio: M4A, WAV. You can also choose resolution (Original, 4K, 1080p, 720p) and playback speed (0.5x–2.0x).

How do AI subtitles work?

SilenceCut uses on-device Whisper AI models to transcribe speech and burn subtitles into your exported video. No internet connection is required.
