Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens entirely on your device. Your files never leave your iPhone or iPad.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app, go to the subscription or settings page, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I get a refund?

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

What devices are supported?

Our apps require iOS 17.0 or later, compatible with iPhone and iPad.
