Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: March 7, 2026

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your account and all associated data from Our App.

1. How to Delete Your Account

Option A: Delete Within the App

  1. Open Our App on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (or tap your profile icon).
  3. Tap "Delete Account" or "Delete My Data".
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted.
  5. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Option B: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the App or the in-app deletion feature, you can request account deletion by sending an email to arthur.dy.lee@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • App name: The name of the App you used.
  • Account identifier: The email address or username associated with your account.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days. In exceptional circumstances (such as additional identity verification requirements, legal compliance reviews, or technical complexity), the processing time may be extended. We will notify you of the estimated completion time in such cases.

Identity Verification: To prevent malicious or unauthorized account deletion requests, we may require you to provide additional identity verification to confirm that you are the legitimate holder of the account. This is consistent with GDPR Article 12(6), which permits identity verification before acting on data subject requests.

2. What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account credentials — email, username, password hash, and authentication tokens.
  • User-generated content — any content you created or uploaded within the App.
  • Local app data — all data stored on your device by the App.
  • Cloud-synced data — if the App uses iCloud (CloudKit) for synchronization, your data stored in iCloud will also be deleted.
  • Preferences and settings — all personalized configurations.
  • Purchase history within the App — records of in-app actions and usage history.

3. What Is NOT Deleted

  • App Store purchase records — Apple retains records of your App Store transactions (downloads, subscriptions, in-app purchases). These are managed by Apple, not by us. To manage subscriptions, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.
  • Anonymized analytics data — aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics that have already been processed cannot be linked back to you and are not subject to deletion.
  • Data required by law — in rare cases, we may be required to retain certain data to comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records, fraud prevention). Such data will be retained only for the legally required period and then deleted.

4. Active Subscriptions

Important: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Apple App Store subscriptions. To avoid continued charges, you must cancel your subscription separately before or after deleting your account:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap your Apple ID (your name at the top).
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select the subscription for Our App and tap Cancel Subscription.

5. Irreversibility

Warning: Account and data deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please make sure to export or back up any data you wish to keep before proceeding with deletion.

6. Re-registration After Deletion

After your account has been deleted, you may register a new account. However, please note:

  • All data previously associated with your deleted account has been permanently removed and cannot be restored to a new account.
  • In-app content or subscription benefits previously associated with the deleted account may not be transferable to a new account (App Store purchase records are managed by your Apple ID and are not affected).
  • The Developer reserves the right to refuse new registration requests for reasonable cause (e.g., if the previous account was suspended or banned for violations of the Terms of Service).

7. Apps Without Accounts

Some of our Apps are standalone tools that do not require account registration and store all data locally on your device. For these Apps:

  • Simply deleting (uninstalling) the App from your device will remove all locally stored data.
  • If the App uses iCloud sync, you can also delete the App's iCloud data via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.
  • No additional account deletion steps are needed.
