1. Information We Collect

Depending on the specific App and features you use, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: If the App offers account registration, we may collect your email address, username, or other credentials you provide during sign-up.

1.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

  • Advertising: If the App displays advertisements, our third-party advertising partners (such as Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers (e.g., IDFA), IP address, and general location data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

Some of our Apps are standalone utility tools that operate entirely on your device. These Apps:

For these Apps, the only data collection that may occur is through third-party advertising SDKs (if the App is ad-supported) and Apple's built-in crash reporting. You can check each App's specific data practices on its App Store product page under the "App Privacy" section.
